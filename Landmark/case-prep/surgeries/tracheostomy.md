## Procedure Snapshot
- **Indication:** Prolonged ventilation or airway obstruction.  
- **Expected duration:** ___  
- **Special instruments:** Tracheostomy set, bronchoscope (for percutaneous).

## Step Workflow — Open
1. Supine with neck extended.  
2. Horizontal skin crease 2 cm above sternal notch.  
3. Divide strap muscles midline; retract thyroid isthmus.  
4. Enter trachea between 2nd–3rd rings; insert tube.  

## Step Workflow — Percutaneous
1. Bronchoscopic visualization; needle entry between 2nd–3rd rings.  
2. Seldinger dilation; insert cuffed tube.  

## Key Pimp Questions
- **Q:** Early vs late complication? Bleeding vs tracheal stenosis (PMID 31376008).  

## Critical Anatomy
- **Landmarks:** Thyroid isthmus, tracheal rings.  
- **Danger zones:** Innominate artery (low trachs).

## Informed Consent Highlights
- Bleeding ___ Pneumomediastinum ___ Tracheal stenosis ___  

## Post-op Considerations
- Humidified air; first tube change > 5 days.
