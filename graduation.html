Eng) in Biodesign within the normal 4 years of traditional medical school. Click the image to the left and watch Dr. Pettigrew tell you about the amazing new program I am starting!

Overall, my experience at USC was amazing. I started a semester late, which at first, I was upset about. In hindsight, I made the best decision! UCLA still sucks, and I feel bad my brother had to go there! I majored in Biophysics as an undergrad and I got the time to explore my intersts. I wrote a blog in my writing class about the physics in science fiction movies. I cant tell you how much better this was than writing book reports on classic literature. Take a look for yourself! I don't think I'll ever have the time to devote my full attention to studying thermodynaics or quantum mechanics, and for that I am grateful.

By far, the best memories over the last five years were with the people I met while at USC. They will be my friends for life. We took many trips, attended countless football games, and enjoyed the amazing SoCal weather. I have to be hoenst, I am not looking forward to the weather in Houston . . .
