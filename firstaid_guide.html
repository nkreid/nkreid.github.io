Here are some tips for how to use your first aid kit!
Please print this out if you plan to use this kit somewhere without cell service

Kit Contents

Medications
- Ibuprofen 200mg (same as Advil and Motrin)
- Acetaminophen 500mg (same as Tylenol)
- Diphenhydramine 25mg (same as Benadryl)
- Loratidine 10mg (same as Claritin)
- Loperamide 2mg (same as Immodium)
- Aspirin 81mg
- Children's Acetaminophen 160mg (same as Tylenol)
- Extra Bags and Labels for your personal medications
Wound Care
- 4x4 Gauze
- Small Bandaids
- Large Bandaids
- Clean Saline Solution
- Triangular Bandage40"x40"x56"
Tapes
- 2" x 10yds Paper Tape
- 4" x 5yds Cohesive Bandage
- 1.5" x 50yds Athletic Tape
Tools
- Tweezers for pulling splinters and other small tasks
- Trauma Shears for cutting clothing
- Nitrile Gloves
